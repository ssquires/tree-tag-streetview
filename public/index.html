Directions (hide)

Click and drag to pan the street view image below and mark the base of every tree you see.

To mark a tree:

1. Click and drag the image to center the red square on the base of the tree, right where the tree meets the ground.

2. Click the "Add Tree" button at the top right of the image.

Click on an existing tree marker to remove it.

If a tree is too far away to see clearly, do not mark it. If the base of the tree is blocked by another object, such as a car, take your best guess of where the base of the tree is.

Use the "Submit HIT" button at the bottom of the page when you're done.
